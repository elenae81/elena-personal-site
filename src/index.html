About

A Spanish creative producer and director with a passion for storytelling that transcends the medium.
She has produced work for clients like Netflix, the British Library and Google.
As a director, she is currently working on a documentary about the Latin American diasporas in South London. Her stories revolve around family, class politics and relationships in worlds estranged from the one we know.
